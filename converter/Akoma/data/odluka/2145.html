The following list contains the classification decisions (other than those subject to a reservation) taken by the Harmonized System Committee (33rd Session – May 2004) on specific products, together with their related Harmonized System code numbers and, in certain cases, the classification rationale.
 
 

Advice

 
Parties seeking to import or export merchandise covered by a decision are advised to verify the implementation of the decision by the importing or exporting country, as the case may be.

Савет

 
Чланицама које желе да увезу или извезу робу обухваћену одлуком, саветује се да провере да ли земља из које увозе или у коју извозе робу примењује одлуку, у зависности од случаја.
